Us

Team committed to sustainability

Our company deals with providing solar solutions to individuals, companies and public bodies. Our mission is to promote a sustainable approach to energy, reducing environmental impact and helping our customers save on energy costs. We deal with various solar services, including the installation of photovoltaic panels, the design and construction of solar thermal systems, the maintenance of existing systems and consulting to choose the best solutions for the specific needs of each client.

Vision

To be leaders in the renewable energy sector, providing innovative and sustainable solar solutions to create a cleaner and safer world for present and future generations.

Mission

To provide efficient and sustainable solar solutions for homes and businesses around the world, empowering people to make the most of the sun's power to reduce their carbon footprint and help build a more sustainable future. We strive to provide high-quality products and services, along with exceptional customer service and a customer satisfaction guarantee.

Services

Facility

Construction, trade, assembly and installation of structures, ballasts and scaffolding for the installation of solar/photovoltaic panels, both on land and at height and in both civil and industrial fields.

Construction

Construction activities in general in any form and aspect, such as the construction and rehabilitation of buildings of any kind for residential use, for industrial, commercial and craft activities, including earthworks.

Mobility

Rental of hot and cold vehicles, such as excavators, wheel loaders, trucks and any other equipment, plant and machinery for construction activities.

Carpentry

Regular cleaning, inspection and maintenance to ensure that your solar panels continue to work efficiently and generate the maximum amount of electricity possible.

Maintenance

We offer trade and installation services for high-quality taps in wood, PVC and aluminum to cover all the needs of our customers.

A bright future

"Solar energy reminds us that nature will always provide us with everything we need if we learn to use it responsibly. Solar panels are one way to do it, and each installation is a step towards a more sustainable and environmentally friendly future".
